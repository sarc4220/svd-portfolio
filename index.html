// Sebastian Victor Dawber — Part I ARB

Designing with data,
building for people

Part I Architectural and Environmental Designer with a specialism in sustainable design and environmental performance analysis. My training covers conceptual drawing through to parametric modelling in Rhinoceros 3D, energy simulation in IES VE, and Python scripting for real environmental data extraction.

My thesis — Back to Basics — examined how mixed-use social housing could be constructed entirely from plant-based renewable materials while addressing socioeconomic deprivation. My competition work uses Python to extract live pollution and environmental data as the generative basis for design.

Outside of work I enjoy photography, music, art, cycling, running, surfing, scuba diving and fitness. Taking ideas from the greats — Frei Otto, Le Corbusier, Buckminster Fuller. My strengths are conceptual thinking, design development, experimentation and drafting.

Currently seeking Part I assistant and architectural technician roles. Available for freelance environmental analysis and visualisation commissions.
